Fundraising Donation

Dear friends,

We are excited to announce that every Sunday, we will be hosting a food sale to raise funds for our church building construction project. This initiative allows us to come together as a community, enjoy delicious food, and contribute to a meaningful cause.

Your participation in the Sunday food sale not only provides you with tasty treats but also helps us take significant steps towards realizing our vision of a new church building. Together, we can make a difference!

Remember the words from the Bible, "Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver." - 2 Corinthians 9:7

Thank you for your continued support and generosity. May God bless you abundantly.
